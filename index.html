About me

I'm a frontend developer and developer advocate.

I build user-friendly web applications and make technology accessible and easier to understand for developers. I believe that the best software is built when developers have access to clear, concise information and a supportive community.

Along the way, I've discovered that I enjoy more than just writing code. I also enjoy sharing knowledge, exploring new tools, and helping other developers navigate their learning journey.

Whether through content, community initiatives or conversations with fellow developers, I'm particularly interested in making technical concepts more approachable and creating spaces where people can learn and grow together.

I'm now making a deliberate move into Developer Relations, looking for a team where I can use both sides of my brain, the one that writes clean codes and the one that connects products with the developers they're built for.
